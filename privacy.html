Privacy Policy for ClipClear

Last updated: November 18, 2025

1. Data Collection and Processing

ClipClear is committed to protecting your privacy. We want you to understand that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your use of the application.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • Photo Library Access: The application requires access to your Photo Library solely for the purpose of identifying, managing (swiping), and deleting screenshot assets. All processing is performed locally on your device. We never access, upload, or transmit your photos or any data related to your photos to our servers.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising services, or other third-party services that collect, store, or share user data. The app is completely self-contained.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our application is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. As we collect no data, COPPA compliance is maintained.
